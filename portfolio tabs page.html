TABS

Tabs is a UX Research Project with the aim to elevate digital journalism. The idea stemmed from my peers and I found it hard to navigate and use the current mainstream media outlets. I imagined it would be easier to provide a familiar platform to the current day user to view, share, and comment on everyday news. This UI design uses the classics of digital journalism like- subscriptions, priority, and news display methods. It refines this service and interaction by providing comment sections, replies, trending, share counters, tags, etc to make it close to its parents. Some of the websites and applications used were Twitter, Instagram, and Reddit.
